Speaking

I believe in the power of knowledge. But to have power, knowledge must be shared. I share my knowledge and passion even with my stutter, because that’s how much I believe.

I have spoken at Confab, An Event Apart, IA Conference, Code for America, and various events in San Francisco and Los Angeles.

As of 2025, I’m available for virtual conferences or events local to the San Francisco Bay Area. Email me for details.
