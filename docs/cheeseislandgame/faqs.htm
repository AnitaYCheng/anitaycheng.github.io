What kind of game is Cheese Island?

Cheese Island is a side-scrolling action/adventure game. You help Rudiger Mouse fly his plane, shoot monsters and pirate mice, and collect good cheese (for points and energy).

How much does Cheese Island cost?

Cheese Island is FREE to download. You can play the first four levels for FREE. The 20 remaining levels are available for 99¢ with in-app purchase.

What is the number next to Rudiger's plane?

Number of arrows you have left. To get more arrows shoot a butterfly wearing a helmet five times and collect the "10x" arrow object that floats up.

What is the little number above the arrows?

Number of points you get if the arrow hits something. The longer the arrow flies the more points it's worth.

I can't find any power-ups. Where are they?

Hint: power-ups come from the tiny bees (small orange bees that come in groups of four).

Why are some levels still locked after I made the in-app-purchase?

Remember you need to complete a level to unlock the next level.
