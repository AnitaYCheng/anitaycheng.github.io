Rudiger Mouse needs your help escaping from the monster-infested Cheese Island!

Rudiger ventured to Cheese Island to find the best cheese in the world! The Cheese Island monsters? Not so happy. Help Rudiger avoid gross green stinky cheese while collecting delicious slices of pepper jack and smoked cheddar!

Features:

  • First 4 levels are FREE!
  • Get 20 more levels for only 99¢ with in-app purchase
  • Huge variety of monsters (including fire-breathing hummingbirds!)
  • Cheese magnet and invincibility power-ups
  • Up/down and side-to-side airplane motion
  • Adjustable tilt controls
  • Optional music and sound FX
  • Complete a level => unlock the next level
