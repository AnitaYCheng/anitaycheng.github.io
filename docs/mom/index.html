A little about Shou-Wen

Born in Taiwan, she met Yun-Chian in graduate school in the US and they had two daughters, Anita and Diane. After working in software, she retired and enjoyed her life by quilting, cross-stitching, gardening, and traveling. And petting all the big dogs she came across!

Things Shou-Wen loved

Animals

Shou-Wen was a lifelong dog lover, especially toward Golden Retrievers. Our rescue dogs Goldie and Tyler were dear members of our family.

She volunteered regularly at Merced's Applegate Park Zoo.

Crafting

Shou-Wen loved to quilt and cross-stitch, but was always open to learning new ways to craft. Her daughter Anita taught her how to knit!

She was an active member of Merced's Gateway Quilters Guild.

Gardening

Shou-Wen planted numerous fruit trees in her backyard in Merced. She enjoyed gifting peaches, nectarines, apricots, and oranges to her friends every year!

Traveling

Shou-Wen traveled extensively in recent years with family and friends. This included trips to the American Southwest, Europe, South America, and Asia.
